SEO Strategy

Building a scalable SEO program from scratch

I joined SingleCare as one of the first SEO hires and helped build the company's SEO program from zero into a core growth channel.

The Challenge

There was no SEO strategy at SingleCare prior to my team joining. I helped design the SEO program alongside the creation of editorial workflows and a full company rebrand - rebuilding SingleCare's digital foundation from the ground up.

The Approach

I built repeatable systems for content quality, trust, and scale:

  • Established cross-functional workflows across editorial, SEO, and design
  • Partnered with editorial and brand teams to create content standards and guidelines
  • Hired and managed specialized writers
  • Implemented medical reviews for E-E-A-T in a highly regulated category
  • Launched core content pillars
  • Partnered with engineering to build an API-driven content system for automatic updates at scale

The Results

25x Organic Growth

In its first three years, SEO became a primary acquisition channel.

Content Strategy

Pivoting to high-intent content in the era of zero-click search

I shifted content strategy away from traffic-first SEO to high-intent, decision-focused content to protect organic revenue amid zero-click and AI-driven SERP changes.

The Challenge

AI Overviews and zero-click SERPs reduced visibility and value of broad high-funnel content, threatening organic performance without necessarily impacting business outcomes.

The Approach

I reoriented content strategy toward mid- to low-funnel topics that answer specific decision-making questions, prioritizing durable content that requires fewer updates while aligning SEO investment directly to conversion and revenue impact.

The Results

8.62% Avg. Conversion Rate
22.39% Top Page Conversion

Lower content maintenance costs with more sustainable revenue contribution.

Technical SEO

Building AI-ready SEO infrastructure for LLM & GEO discoverability

I led technical SEO initiatives to ensure content was discoverable, rendered, and visible in AI answer engines.

The Challenge

As AI-powered search and non-traditional user agents emerged, JavaScript-heavy components and inconsistent structured data limited reliable rendering and content extraction across key commercial pages.

The Approach

I partnered with engineering to:

  • Implement JavaScript fallbacks
  • Automate and expand schema markup across commercial pages
  • Improve core web vitals
  • Built a dedicated directory to test GEO strategies

The Results

  • Improved rendering and crawlability across critical page templates
  • Expanded structured data coverage across commercial content
  • Increased the number of commercial pages receiving AI referral traffic following technical improvements
  • Created a framework for evaluating GEO strategies as the channel evolves
